Eggplant Pasta

Ingridients

Eggplant
Yellow onion
Garlick
Pasta
Tomato suace
Spices
Oil

Steps

  1. Lay chopped eggplant and onion on a sheet pan and toss with with oil, salt, and garlic powder. Roasted 400F for 20-25 minutes - flip halfway through.
  2. While eggplant is roasting, bring pot of well salted water to a boil for pasta. Cook noodles until “al dente” in texture.
  3. Then heat up sauce in a pan and fold in roasted eggplant and onions.
  4. Serve sauce with pasta noodles and fresh basil.
